🌍 Base view
The scatterplot keeps the global overview of the displayed Goodreads books.

🔬 Local Analysis Lens
The lens creates a temporary local view without changing the global scatterplot outside the lens.

🧩 Lens functions
Suppress: the lens hides books inside the lens that do not match the active genre, rating or page filters. This is the clutter-reduction effect used by Sampling Lens examples.
Alter: inside the lens, point sizes are temporarily re-encoded relative to the local lens region. Books above the local rating average get a green outline; books below it get an orange outline.
Enrich: the lens adds local statistics plus detail cards for the focused book and the best candidate.

🏆 Best candidate
The upper card shows the strongest local candidate. The score combines average rating, popularity and a preference for medium-length books.

🎯 Focus book
The lower card shows the book closest to the lens center.

⚙️ Performance slider
The update threshold controls how many pixels the mouse must move before the lens redraws. Choose a higher value on slower PCs; choose a lower value for a more direct lens feeling.

📦 Focus region
Draw a rectangle to create a second detailed view below the main scatterplot.
